Swapno Puron Amtala

Spread approx. 32 Bigha of land, Swapno Puron Amtala, phase -1, 2 & 3 are the first gated community residential project in Amtala, D.H Road by prestigious and renowned developers Srijan & Star Group. Swapno Puron is an affordable project situated in Amtala, D.H Road, 10 KM from upcoming Joka Metro.

The price of the flats under this project vary between INR 9.50 Lac - INR 27.10 Lac. Here, every space embodies joy and comfort and every facility infuses a spirit of celebration. There are 756 apartments in 21 blocks with G+4 floors with Lift & 5 Unique Row Houses.

Swapno Puron Amtala has enough green space and a 3 -acre private natural lake with boating and fishing facility and a spectacular wooden fishing deck for refreshments. It has modern club house with all amenities for residents and a grocery chop, parlor & medicine shop with in complex .
